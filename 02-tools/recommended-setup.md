# My Setup

The honest version, as of late April 2026.

I use **[Claude Code](./claude-code.md)** as the primary harness — agentic multi-file work, refactoring, codebase exploration, security review passes. It's where I spend the bulk of my AI coding time. With serious daily use I pay roughly **$200/month** at current usage-billed pricing (Anthropic's published benchmark for serious users is ~$13/dev/active day, which lands in this range).

I keep **[Cursor](./cursor.md)** for IDE-native interactive editing and inline completion. About **$60/month**.

Total: **about $260/month** of personal tooling.

Pricing shifted from flat per-seat to usage-billed-on-top in early 2026; serious daily Claude Code use lands in the $150-250/month range. If you're an occasional user the math is lighter; if you're heavier than me it's higher.

I think the price is justified for my level of usage. The honest case for it: a senior engineer hour costs more than a month of these tools, and the tooling reliably saves me at least a few hours a week. The honest counter: at less-intensive usage levels the ROI is marginal, and the per-seat case for a junior engineer or occasional user is harder to make.

For the broader cost picture (including the operational costs that dwarf the subscription line item — review time, incident response, scanning infrastructure), see [my experience page](../01-foundations/my-experience.md#what-it-actually-costs).
