# Quality

This folder is about **quality**: the discipline of catching mistakes, debugging unfamiliar bugs, and maintaining systems over time when most of the new code is being written by AI. Security is its own concern and lives in [09 — Security](../09-security/), the failure modes are different enough that bundling them together blurred both.

The thesis: AI raises the cost of low quality more than it raises the cost of low velocity. The teams that win are the ones that invest more in quality discipline as their generation rate goes up, not less.
