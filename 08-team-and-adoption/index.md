Two audiences, one folder. Read the section that matches your altitude; the other is one click away when you need it.

This is the team-of-5-to-15 view. What changes when you scale from "me and AI" to "my team and AI": training, code review, junior pipeline, the alignment problem that surfaces at month three.

- [Measuring impact](./measuring-impact.md), DORA over self-report, the hidden cost of PR review
- [For team leads](./for-team-leads.md), 90-day rollout plan, training, review guidelines, escalation boundaries
- [Junior developers](./junior-developers.md), pair first and AI later, with concrete skill gates by month
- [The alignment bottleneck](./alignment-bottleneck.md), why team throughput hasn't moved as much as individual throughput, and what to do today

## If you're a CTO/VPE/Director making org-level decisions

This is the rollout problem at scale: maturity, governance, ROI, org design, the 90-day plan. It assumes the underlying ideas from the rest of the guide.

- [AI coding maturity model](./maturity-model.md), the opinionated 6-level model with assessment criteria
- [Risk, governance, policy](./risk-governance-policy.md), STRIDE-style threat model, EU AI Act mapping, AUP framing
- [ROI and the case for investment](./roi-and-board-narrative.md), DX Core 4, TCO modeling, the bimodal productivity story for the boardroom
- [Org design + platform team](./org-design.md), platform team scope, AI champions network, hiring/leveling
- [The 90-day roadmap](./90-day-roadmap.md), concrete plans per starting maturity level
- [Case studies](./case-studies.md), real outcomes (Booking, Replit, Klarna, Cloudflare, Shopify, Apiiro, Anthropic) — what worked and what didn't
- 📊 [Maturity assessment](./assessment.md), 10 questions, get your level plus actions *(site only)*
- [Templates](./templates/), AUP, org-level AGENTS.md, code review checklist, board update slides, incident-response runbook
