## What this guide is

A practitioner's notebook from two years of daily AI coding work. Opinionated, current as of April 2026, and structured so you can read just the parts that match what you're trying to do.

It's not a vendor pitch. It calls out what doesn't work as carefully as what does — vibe coding's failure modes, the Java security paradox, the alignment bottleneck nobody talks about, the prompts that produce production bugs. And it's written in first-person practitioner voice, not consultant voice.
